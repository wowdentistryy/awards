@WOW DENTISTRY

CALL FOR PRESENTERS

WOW DENTISTRY Congress is a one-of-a-kind event that unites the most influential players and advocates in the women’s dental community to showcase and exchange their work on a global scale. We welcome submissions from renowned scientists, researchers, experienced academicians, and anyone who believes in empowering women by supporting fundraising activities.

The call for abstracts ran from JANUARY 30, 2024 until APRIL 30, 2024.

Here are six compelling reasons to submit your abstract and become a part of the WOW program.

  • Present your work at an exclusive women’s congress and accelerate progress in the field.
  • Share your expertise with a diverse, multi-sectoral audience and gain recognition for your achievements.
  • Sketch your struggles and experience, your organization and your impactful achievements on a powerful platform.
  • Share your personal journey as a professional, entrepreneur, researcher, motivator, woman, daughter, sister, spouse, mother, or any other role you may play.
  • Connect with fellow professionals and gain valuable feedback and new insights.
  • Exchange best practices and establish meaningful connections with colleagues from all around the world.

Submission Guidelines

  • →All submissions should be made in English and only through the online submission platform.
  • →Abstract proposal must be Word document with updated CV and recent photo along with the past presentation AV.
  • →Abstracts submitted in another language than English will be automatically rejected.
  • →The abstract must contain at least 1,500 characters and cannot exceed 3,000 characters (including spaces, punctuation, authors' affiliation, and titles).
  • →Prescribed format for submission types:
    • ♦Scientific studies: Background, aim, methods, results, conclusions.
    • ♦Practice, policy, and advocacy reports: Background and context, aim, strategy/tactics, programme/policy process, outcomes, lessons learned (including replicability potential).
  • →Ensure abstract is error-free and meets international ethical standards.
  • →Define all abbreviations used.
  • →Include commented graphics and visuals with explanatory titles.
  • →Declare all authors' conflicts of interest for the last 5 years.
  • →Authors must authorize the submission and presentation of the abstract.
  • →Mention all sources of information and references.
  • →Confirmation email with abstract ID will be provided upon submission.
  • →Abstracts can be edited until final submission deadline: April 30, 2024, at midnight PST.
